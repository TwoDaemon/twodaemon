About this site

This is my personal website.

In theory that means I could put all kinds of arbitrary crap on it. In reality, it's pretty likely to be mostly game and game design discussion and analysis, since those're topics about which I have the most to say.

The most to say in some kind of authoritative manner, anyway. I actually like talking about nearly anything, but give me a soapbox and I guess I talk about games.

Welcome to my soapbox.

Who am I?

Actually, a better question might be "Why should you listen to anything I say?"

Because I think before I speak and I try to understand and explain my chosen topics clearly, and I like to think I do a pretty good job.

Failing that, I suggest you make your own decisions.

In a personal sense, I'm a programmer working for a mid-sized web development company in Sheffield, England.

I like to play, think about, talk about and (occasionally) write about games and game design. That's both board and video games, incidentally.

I can be reached most easily on Twitter at @TwoDaemon and via my email at tkitchin AT gmail DOT com.
