Journey

2013-07-16 01:16

So I finally borrowed a PS3 and took the opportunity to try out Journey. I'm aware I'm a touch late to this particular party, but I suspect Journey is timeless.

Journey is like...

No.

Journey is making a trusted friend, through thick and thin, with all the joy and pain that implies.

I can think of no higher praise, and I would recommend it to anyone and everyone.
